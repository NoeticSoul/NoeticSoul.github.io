Projects & Case Studies

Work framed as practical proof of capability

You do not need giant software projects for a strong portfolio. These case studies turn your existing experience into concrete examples employers can quickly understand.

Enterprise User Support

Context: University enterprise office environment supporting 80–100 users.

Focus: Hardware and software troubleshooting, account access issues, connectivity problems, and responsive front-line support.

Value: Demonstrates professionalism, consistency, and the ability to keep end users productive in a structured environment.

Imaging and Deployment Workflow

Context: Standardized system setup and secure provisioning for user devices.

Focus: Imaging, deployment assistance, office setup, network-connected device readiness, and handoff quality.

Value: Shows operational discipline and readiness for endpoint-focused support teams.

Support Documentation Improvement

Context: Internal support and conference room procedures needed stronger consistency.

Focus: Imaging, onboarding, troubleshooting, and room-use documentation that improved knowledge transfer.

Value: Supports the 45% reduction in average ticket resolution time through better internal workflow clarity.
